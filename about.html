About Antidote

Chemical-free poison oak removal and ecological restoration: our approach and our values

Our Mission

Antidote was born out of a simple frustration: most poison oak removal relies on herbicides that don't actually work. They burn back what's visible above ground, but the root survives, and the plant comes right back. We set out to do it differently.

We remove poison oak entirely by hand, root and all. No chemical sprays, no herbicides, no toxic runoff into your soil or watershed. It's more labor-intensive than reaching for a bottle, but it's the only approach that clears the problem for good and leaves the land around it genuinely unharmed.

We believe that caring for your property shouldn't come at the expense of the ecosystem it sits in. The East Bay's hillsides, waterways, and native habitats matter to us, and every job we take on reflects that.

  • 100% chemical-free — no herbicides, no sprays, no exceptions
  • Committed to ecological restoration, not just surface removal
  • Trained in safe plant handling and disposal
  • Familiar with local terrain and plant varieties
  • Satisfaction guaranteed on every job
  • Locally owned and operated

The Restoration Approach

No chemicals. No shortcuts. Real restoration, from the root up.

Zero Chemicals

We never use herbicides or chemical sprays. Beyond the environmental harm, they simply don't solve the problem, killing only what's above ground. The only real fix is pulling the whole root out, not poisoning the soil around it.

Root-Deep Removal

Every plant comes out at the root. We dig down, extract the full root system, and haul all material off-site, leaving your soil clean and intact with nothing left behind to regrow.

Safe & Restored

Because everything is done by hand with no chemicals or residue, your family and pets can use the space immediately. Your local waterways, birds, and soil biology are left intact and healthier for it.
